Marlvoch's first instinct had been to smash through the human fleet, yet he realized he didn't have near enough firepower, and to even attempt to do so would be insane. As such, he turned around and fled without so much as a second thought. Even as he entertained the thought of the vast cheerings of the crew as he vanquished the human slime, he thought it would be much better to go away with his life.

“Captain,” a crew member addressed Marlvoch.

“Yes?”

“We have received word from Master Rujickmoli. He is currently docking at the Proxima Base. Shall we go there to greet him?”

“Yes, of course, dumbass! Go! And don't forget the gooturibeeducken!”

The Goblin Captain thought a moment, and then sat back in his chair. He really had no idea what to do.
